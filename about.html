Bio

“It’s through mistakes that you actually can grow. You have to get bad in order to get good.” - Paula Scher

I am a UX/UI designer with a passion for design, code, and art. I have the unique ability to harness both sides of my brain because I am both analytical and artistic.

Throughout my career, I have held multiple roles requiring wearing multiple hats. I have worked as a Business Analyst, Operations Analyst, and Data Analyst working with various companies including small start-ups, non-profits, and large corporate enterprises. I have worked on projects across many cross-functional teams designing and testing applications and analyzing large sets of data with advanced MS Excel techniques and SQL queries. I have worked alongside Operations Managers, Marketing Managers, Product Managers, UX/UI Designers, and Developers both locally and remotely.

Software design trends are constantly changing, the companies that iterate rapidly and produce better UX/UI experiences will gain and retain a competitive advantage. It is paramount that companies hire motivated UX/UI Designers that have a passion and understanding for design, technology, business, and market trends to adapt to changes.
